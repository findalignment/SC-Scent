# Policies

## Shipping Policy

### Processing Time
Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays).

### Shipping Methods
- **Standard Shipping**: 3-5 business days
- **Priority Shipping**: 2-3 business days
- **Free Shipping**: Available on orders over $75 (standard shipping only)

### Tracking
You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

## Return & Exchange Policy

### Returns
Due to the personal nature of fragrances, we cannot accept returns on opened items. Unopened items in original condition may be returned within 14 days of delivery for a full refund minus shipping costs.

### Damaged or Incorrect Items
If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 48 hours at hello@santacruzscent.com with photos. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund.

### How to Return
1. Email hello@santacruzscent.com with your order number
2. Wait for return authorization
3. Ship items back in original packaging
4. Refunds processed within 5-7 business days of receipt
